PuVAE: A Variational Autoencoder to Purify Adversarial Examples

A Puried Images from PuVAE
Figures 1 and 2 are the visualizations of the experimental results on the MNIST and Fashion-MNIST datasets respectively. In
each image, the left is the image attacked with FGSM(0.3), and the right is the puried result of the left image using the PuVAE.
In both datasets, the adversarial noise is selectively removed while preserving the original shape of the image. Particularly, the
PuVAE puries adversarial examples while maintaining the angle of the number 1 in the rst row of Figure 1.
Figure 1: Examples of puried images from PuVAE on MNIST dataset.
Figure 2: Examples of puried images from PuVAE using Fashion-MNIST dataset.
